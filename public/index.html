Welcome

This is the place for recording your feats of hand/eye coordination, showing the entire world your prowess in foosballness.

Two kinds of leagues are run: singles and doubles. Singles is for one-on-one matches, where one player plays the red team, while the other player plays blue. Doubles matches have two players on each side, taking turns playing the offensive and defensive rods.

Bug-reports and suggestions should be routed to Vidar Larsen.
